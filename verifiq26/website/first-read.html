§ 04 · questions
Why isn't it free?

Because free trials get farmed with throwaway emails and tell us nothing. €29 on a work card means you're a real practice with a real pack — and it gets you a real result, not a demo. This is the first-pack rate, not the rate card.

What do you need from me?

For the €29 First Read, up to 25 drawings from one discipline. For the full power, upload everything — drawings, the technical and general specifications, the particulars, and the form of contract. The more complete the pack, the more the cross-reference finds.

Why a work email?

This is a tool for practising design teams, and we keep it that way. Work email only, one First Read per practice — no anonymous sign-ups, no abuse.

Is this giving me design advice?

No. VerifIQ is a reading aid. It surfaces, in the documents' own words, what a registered professional may wish to read closely. It does not certify, sign, or opine. You read it, you exercise your judgement, you verify locally, you sign.
