— III. One Finding, in Full

Our method, on a single example.

Rev · A · Critical
Critical Finding · C-03 Discipline · Architecture / Contract Reviewer · A. Ní Cheallaigh, RIAI

Date for Substantial Completion is left blank.

The Date for Substantial Completion is a required entry in the Form of Tender Schedule, Part 1, Section 4.2. Without a specified date, the Liquidated Damages mechanism set out in Clause 9.5 of PW-CF5 cannot be enforced. This is a material defect in the tender pack — bidders cannot price contract programme risk and the Employer cannot enforce damages for delay.

"Date for Substantial Completion: ____________" Form of Tender · Schedule Part 1 · §4.2 · Page 12
Recommended action

Insert Date for Substantial Completion before tender release. Confirm with the Employer's Representative and re-issue the Form of Tender Schedule Part 1 as Revision B.
