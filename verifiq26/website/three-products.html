If you're not sure which.

A short decision tree. The wizard works the rest out from your replies — see Request the brief.

"I'm on the design team — multi-discipline, pre-tender."
Use VERIFY — the multi-discipline cross-pass is the point.
"I'm a contractor pricing a tender."
Use HUNT — exposure band and tactical RFI list, not a coordination tool.
"I'm a sole practitioner signing one discipline."
Use STUDIO — lighter touch, one discipline, right-sized.
"I'm reviewing one discipline of a big team's pack."
Use STUDIO — nominate your discipline; we only read that.
"I'm a QS doing pre-issue BoQ coordination on a multi-discipline pack."
Use VERIFY — the multi-discipline cross-pass surfaces BoQ vs spec mismatches.
"I'm an Assigned Certifier checking doc-set hygiene."
Use VERIFY for multi-discipline AC prep; use STUDIO for single-discipline. Note: VerifIQ does not replace AC function.
"Tender programme is huge — over 600 docs."
Use VERIFY Tier IV (Programme) or Tier V (Mega). Concierge route.
"I just want to see what one of these looks like."
Free taster on any product — one discipline, 20 docs, counts plus one worked finding.
