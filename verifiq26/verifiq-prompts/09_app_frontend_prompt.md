## In-app explanation block

Use this on the dashboard, on the brief request, and on the free taster page.

```
Upload your design-team documents.

VerifIQ will convene a virtual Pre-Build Compliance Council
across your project disciplines.

Each discipline reviews its own information, challenges the
others, removes weak or duplicated findings, and returns one
coordinated build-readiness decision.

VerifIQ checks:

  · Architecture
  · Fire safety
  · Accessibility / DAC
  · Mechanical
  · Electrical
  · Structural
  · Civil
  · Planning
  · Landscape
  · Conservation
  · PSDP
  · Assigned Certifier / BCAR
  · Quantity Surveying
  · Contractor buildability
  · Specialist systems
  · Sector regulators where relevant

The output is not a checklist.
It is a structured decision:

  · Proceed
  · Proceed with conditions
  · Pause before build
  · Insufficient information
```

## Voice guidance

- Sober, direct, no marketing fluff.
- Sentence case, never title case.
- No emojis.
- Avoid the verbs in `08_guardrails.md` banned list.
- Speak to chartered professionals as peers, not as targets.
