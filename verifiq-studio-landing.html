// Honest pitch. No fluff.

The bigger checking tools and independent peer reviews run €5-15k per pack. If you're a sole practitioner doing two or three projects a year, that math doesn't work.

VerifIQ Studio is €60-€300 for a single-discipline check. Catches the document-quality stuff your trainees would have caught — if you had any. Outdated standard references, schedule mismatches, fire-rating conflicts with TGD-B, cut-paste from a previous project.

It's not a chartered review. It's not a BCAR Ancillary Cert. It's not professional advice.

It's a sanity check before you issue. €60-€300 to maybe catch something annoying before your client does.
