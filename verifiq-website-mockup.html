Source · Electrical Specification §12.20

12.20 LIGHTNING PROTECTION SYSTEM

The lightning protection system shall be designed in accordance with BS 7430 and BS EN 62305. The installation is for the proposed School Type Building at Convent Hill, Ballina, Co. Mayo being a 2-storey structure approximately 11.0m high.

The system shall comprise air termination network, down conductors, and earth termination network in compliance with the architectural drawings prepared by SJK Architects.

Finding · CRITICAL
E-103

Wrong-project cut-paste in Lightning Protection spec.

Spec §12.20 describes a 2-storey school in Co. Mayo (Convent Hill, Ballina) with a different architect (SJK). The actual project is a single-storey day-centre in Dublin. The entire lightning protection design intent has been imported from a different project without project-specific adaptation. Please verify locally and request a re-issue from the M&E designer.

Elec Spec · p.78 · §12.20
